Resturant Description
This is your go-to place for our famous Italian Sausage and Peppers as well as other favorites such as burger and fries, hot dog and chips, and our house salad. Enjoying a burger and fries is one of the many ways you can show your Minnesota pride while having the Best Day Ever. The burger features pickles, onion, tomato, and lettuce on top of a fresh beef patty. Having a meal at Northwoods Grill is like having a backyard cookout, but without the cleanup! Everything here is fresh from the grill and sure to please. If you haven’t stopped by yet, what’s stopping you?
